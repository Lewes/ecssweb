Events

ECSS runs numerous social events throughout the year for its members. These range from industry events with top employers to nightlife events (alcohol is not mandatory!) to board game evenings.

The timeline below shows some of our "highlight" events we've had and have planned for 2021/22. We will also be running smaller events so that there will be at least one event per week. You can check what events are on at our Facebook page as well as our Discord.

JumpStart 2021

Welcome freshers! During this packed week, we would'ave introduced you to ECSS with a large selection of events including the yearly freshers' City Challenge, ECS Bunfight, the big "Pizza and Booze" Social, and finishing the week with our first micro (10h) Hackathon of the year

27th Sept. 2021

Halloween House Crawl

Touring three committee houses with decorations and drinks at each stop, everyone dressed up in super scary costumes on one of our biggest socials!

31st Oct. 2021

Video Game Social

Everyone gets together in the labs to eat pizza and play some video games - casual and competitive. There are also board games available if video games aren't your fancy.

November 2021

November Hackaton

Flex your programming talent for exciting prizes in our 24 hour hackathon challenge. Would recommend to anyone who loves to code, or to show off

November 2021

White T-Shirt Social

We are going through all the top pubs in Portswood - a fun night of drinking and writing nonesense on eachother's t-shirts

December 2021

Christmas Party

Party in style at our annual Christmas do! Dress to kill and show off your dancing 'skills'.

December 2021

The Big ECSS GameJam

Show off both your creativity and code skills during our February Game Jam.

February 2022

Flip Out Social

Flip Out Southampton's Over 18s Night include jumping around their massive trampoline park with LIVE Drum and Bass

April Hackathon

Our third Hackathon of the year. Once again, a good chance to flex and win prizes

April 2022

Hobbit BBQ

Top off your exam season with food on us! Also hosted at the Hobbit, come hangout with your friends in the sun with an abundance of free burgers and hot dogs.

May 2022

Summer Ball

Round off the year with our fancy ball! Smart attire, posh food and delicious cocktails to get some quality social media posts.

June 2022

Note: the plan shown in the timeline IS NOT LIMITED and may, in fact, be subject to change. We always welcome new cool ideas from society members!

You can always come back to our website or follow us on Facebook for updates and checkout our events information.

Please note that photography and filming might be taking place at our events and ECSS will be using them. Please let us know if you do not want to be photographed/filmed. Find out more in our media notice.
