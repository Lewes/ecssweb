Events

ECSS runs numerous social events throughout the year for its members. These range from industry events with top employers to nightlife events (alcohol is not mandatory!) to board game evenings.

The timeline below shows some of our top events we've had and have planned for 2019/20. We will also be running smaller events so that there will be at least one event per week. You can check what events are on at our Facebook page.

Freshers' Social

Welcome freshers! On this evening, we introduced you to ECSS with 140 free pizzas and a crawl through Portswood.

24th Sept. 2019

Video Game Social

Everyone gets together in the labs to eat pizza and play some video games - casual and competitive. There are also board games available if video games aren't your fancy.

3rd Oct. 2019

White T-Shirt Social

A traditional country-wide form of social, involving a bar crawl with challenges and writing nonsense on each other's t-shirts.

4th Oct. 2019

Oceana Fire Social

With everyone dressing up in red clothing, we invaded Wetherspoons and then Oceana for our traditional trip to Oceana.

9th Oct. 2019

Halloween House Crawl

Touring three committee houses with decorations and drinks at each stop, everyone dressed up in super scary costumes on one of our biggest socials!

25th Oct. 2019

Smash Bros Tournament

Spreading over 3 Nintendo Switches, a huge tournament of Smash bros took place with free pizza as always!

14th Nov. 2019

Beach Party

In the cold of winter, we gathered in The Hobbit with shorts, leis, beer pong and birthday cake for our society president.

19th Nov. 2019

Christmas Party

Party in style at our annual Christmas do! Dress to kill and show off your dancing 'skills'.

Dec. 2019

Hackathon

Flex your programming talent for exciting prizes in our 24 hour hackathon challenge. Would recommend to anyone who loves to code.

Feb. 2020

Hobbit BBQ

Top off your exam season with food on us! Also hosted at the Hobbit, come hangout with your friends in the sun with an abundance of free burgers and hot dogs.

Jun. 2020

Boat Ball

Round off the year with a fancy ball on a boat! Smart attire, posh food and delicious cocktails to get some quality social media posts.

Jun. 2020

Note: the plan shown in the timeline may be subject to change.

You can always come back to our website or follow us on Facebook for updates and checkout our events information.

Please note that photography and filming might be taking place at our events and ECSS will be using them. Please let us know if you do not want to be photographed/filmed. Find out more in our media notice.
