Others

Compete for the title of best player in ECSS! We have annual ladders in Table tennis, badminton, pool and squash. The ladders are set up in the first few weeks but anyone can join at any time.

How it works

Players challenge each other no higher than 2 places above them, they play a best of 3 game, and report back to the league. If the challenger wins, they are moved above the player they challenged.
